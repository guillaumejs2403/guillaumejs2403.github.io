About me

Guillaume Jeanneret Sanmiguel, M.Sc.

Brief Summary

I am a Biomedical Engineer from la Universidad de los Andes. I obtained my Master's Degree in Biomedical Engineering at the Universidad de los Andes.I've worked as a Graduate Research Assistant at the CinfonIA research group, led by Dr. Pablo Arbelaez. Currently, I am a PhD student at Unicaen under the supervision of Frédéric Jurie and Loic Simon.

A little bit about my life

I have lived all my life in Bogota, Colombia, a beautiful country in South America. Colombia is known for its coffee, its immense flora and fauna, and its lovely people. Born to a Swiss father and a Colombian mother, I went to a Swiss school where I learned French.

I decided to study biomedical engineering because it was a mix of medicine and mathematics. Halfway through my undergraduate studies, I fell in love with machine learning and research. I decided to continue on this path and worked with Pablo Arbelaez in my master's program, where I improved my research skills.
